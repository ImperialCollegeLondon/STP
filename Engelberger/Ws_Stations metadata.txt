Ws_Stations.mat
---
Ws_Stations.name - Station name, {string}.
Ws_Stations.elevation - elevation [m a.s.l.], {scalar}.
Ws_Stations.Time - Matlab date format, {N x 1 vector}.
Ws_Stations.Ws - Wind speed at station level; hourly mean [m s^-1], {N x 1 vector}.

N - Sample size.

For Engelberger catchment- Data were derived from the MeteoSwiss hourly station data for the period 1999-2012.
