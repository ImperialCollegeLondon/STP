NHRO.mat
---
NHRO.occurence - Mean number of days above a certain threshold {Y x P}. 

Y - Years
P - number of pixels in the domain

For Engelberger catchment- NHRO was derived from daily MeteoSwiss gridded rainfall data, 2-km resolution, for the period 1981-2012.
A day is recorded as wet exceeding a rainfall threshold of 1 mm per day.
