Rsw_Stations.mat
---
Rsw_Stations.name - Station name, {string}.
Rsw_Stations.elevation - elevation [m a.s.l.], {scalar}.
Rsw_Stations.Time - Matlab date format, {N x 1 vector}.
Rsw_Stations.VP - Vapor pressure [hPa], {N x 1 vector}.

N - Sample size.

For Engelberger catchment- Data were derived from the MeteoSwiss hourly station data for the period 1985-2014.
