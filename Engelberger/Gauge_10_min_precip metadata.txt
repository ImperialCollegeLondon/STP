MERGED - Rainfall 10-min time series (1-rain, 0-no rain) for the Engelberger domian, composed of 4 rain gauges surronding the domain. First vector is boolean and second vector is date [matlab format].

ALT, ENG, LUZ and PIL - Data of the 4 rain gauges from which the MERGED vector was derived for the period 1983-2013 (including)
