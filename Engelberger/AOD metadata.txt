AOD.mat
---
AOD.Time - Matlab date format, {N x 1 vector}.
AOD.Data- Aerosol optical depth (AOD) for the different wavelengths [-], {W x N vector}.

N - Sample size.
W- wavelengths [1020, 870, 675, 500, 440, 380, 340]

For Engelberger catchment- Data were derived from the AERONET station data for the period 2003-2014.
http://aeronet.gsfc.nasa.gov/
