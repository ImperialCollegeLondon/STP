DP_Stations.mat
---
DP_Stations.name - Station name, {string}.
DP_Stations.Time - Matlab date format, {N x 1 vector}.
DP_Stations.DP - Dew point temperature [deg. C], {N x 1 vector}.

N - Sample size.

For Engelberger catchment- Data were derived from the MeteoSwiss hourly station data for the period 2006-2016.
