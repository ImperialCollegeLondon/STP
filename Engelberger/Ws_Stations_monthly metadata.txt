Ws_Stations.mat
---
Ws_Stations.name - Station name, {string}.
Ws_Stations.elevation - elevation [m a.s.l.], {scalar}.
Ws_Stations.z0 - roughness [m], {N x 1 vector}.
Ws_Stations.Wind - Wind speed at station level; monthly mean [m s^-1], {N x 1 vector}.

N - Sample size.

For Engelberger catchment- Data were derived from the MeteoSwiss station data for the period 1999-2015 obtained from http://wind-data.ch/
